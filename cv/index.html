I explore the intersection of life sciences and custom scientific instrumentations, with a primary focus on designing, buildling, and disseminating optical neurotechnologies. Through this pursuit, I aim to aid discovery as well as identify where these tools fall short of expectations.
My habitable zone = present-day technological utlity coupled with active engagement for further refinements + an opportunity to venture into uncharted territories
My work is driven by curiosity and inspired by open science

Research Interests

  • Optics, Imaging, Microscopy
  • Light-sheet microscopy, Optical coherence imaging
  • Neuronal calcium activity and voltage imaging
  • Surface plasmons, Plasmonic gold nanoparticles
  • Nanoscale diffusion, complex fluids, viscoelasticity, optical rheology
